Mammut

A highspeed gravity racer



SETTING

You are a mammoth. The cave you have been living in is collapsing, and you have to run and make your way out. You cannot stop, you can only run faster and faster towards your escape.

But how do you manage your way through the blocks of stone scattered across the cave? You cannot jump, not with the seven tons you are weighing. Luckily, you can change the gravity to fall on the next block and make your way forward.

But beware, do not fall off your path; falling onto the cave might end deadly. Do not crush into a block or your escape is over. Do not hit the walls seperating the cave's segments, they only impede you on your progress.

Can you make it to the end of the cave?

INSPIRATIONS

Mammut takes the gravity switch from Gravity Guy, the endless escape from Canabalt and the high speed from Race the Sun and puts these ideas into an entirely new setting and an art style inspired by the Woolly Mammoth by Timothy J. Reynolds.
